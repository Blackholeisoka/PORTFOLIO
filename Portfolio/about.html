Hello! I'm Melvin, a first-year student at ESIEA on the Agen campus (Class 14). Passionate about computer science from a young age, I decided to turn this passion into a career by training as a self-taught developer.

My journey has allowed me to develop solid skills in back-end development with technologies like ExpressJS, Node.js, PHP, C#, and databases like MySQL and PostgreSQL. I love creating robust and scalable server-side applications. Each project is an opportunity to learn and challenge myself.

Beyond web development, I'm very interested in cybersecurity and pentesting. I regularly participate in CTF competitions to sharpen my skills. This dual expertise allows me to have a comprehensive view of security in my developments.
